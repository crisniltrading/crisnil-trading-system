Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from CRISNIL

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order varies by product. For wholesale orders, we typically require a minimum of 10kg per product type. Contact us for specific requirements.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver across Region 4 (CALABARZON) and the National Capital Region (NCR). Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 10 AM.

How do you ensure product quality?

All products are stored at -18°C in our FDA-approved facilities. We use temperature-controlled vehicles and maintain strict cold chain protocols throughout delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, checks, and cash on delivery for verified business accounts. Credit terms are available for qualified customers.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once logged in, you can track your order status in real-time through our dashboard. You'll receive notifications at each stage of delivery.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Absolutely! We offer competitive pricing for bulk orders. The more you order, the better the rates. Contact our sales team for custom quotes.
